Chicken Satay with Peanut Sauce

Description

Chicken satay is a popular Southeast Asian dish that consists of marinated and skewered chicken pieces grilled to perfection. Originating from Indonesia, this delectable street food has gained worldwide recognition for its tantalizing flavours and aromatic spices. This recipe for chicken satay with peanut sauce features flavorful Thai-style chicken skewers and a sweet and spicy peanut sauce.

Ingredients

  • 1 1⁄4 cup unsweetened coconut milk, divided
  • 5 teaspoons yellow curry powder, divided
  • 4 teaspoons white sugar, divided
  • 4 teaspoons fish sauce, divided
  • 1⁄2 teaspoon granulated garlic
  • 1 pound skinless, boneless chicken breasts, cut into strips
  • 8 skewers
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1⁄2 cup chicken broth
  • 1⁄4 cup creamy peanut butter
  • 1 tablespoon lime juice
