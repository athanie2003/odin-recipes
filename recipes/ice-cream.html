Ice Cream Sandwich Cake

Description

Ice cream sandwich cake is a delightful dessert that combines the best of both worlds: ice cream and cake. This indulgent treat is perfect for those who love the creamy, cool texture of ice cream and the moist, spongy goodness of cake.

Ingredients

  • 24 vanilla ice cream sanwiches, unwrapped
  • 2 containers whipped topping
  • 1 jar hot fudge ice cream topping, warmed
  • 1 jar caramel ice cream topping
  • 1⁄4 cup chopped pecans, or to taste
