Lasagna

Description

Indulge in the ultimate comfort food with this mouthwatering lasagna recipe. Layered with love and bursting with flavours, this classic Italian dish is a perfect blend of tender pasta sheets, rich tomateo sauce, savoury ground meat, and a medley of melted cheeses.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound sweet Italian Sausage
  • 3⁄4 pound lean ground beef
  • 1⁄2 cup minced onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1 can of crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cans tomato sauce
  • 2 cans tomato paste
  • 1⁄2 cup water
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 4 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley, divided
  • 1 1⁄2 teaspoons dried basil leaves
  • 1 1⁄2 teaspoons salt, divided, or to taste
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • 1⁄2 teaspoons fennel seeds
  • 1⁄4 teaspoons ground black pepper
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 16 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 3⁄4 pound mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • 3⁄4 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna in the following order:
    1. Meat Sauce
    2. Noodles
    3. Ricotta mixture
    4. Mozzarella slices
    5. Meat sauce
    6. Parmesan cheese
    7. Repeat and top with remaining Parmesan
  5. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes at 375 degrees F.
  6. Remove foil and bake for 25 minutes at 375 degrees F.
  7. Let lasagna rest at room temperature for 15 minutes before serving.
