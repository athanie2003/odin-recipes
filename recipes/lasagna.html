Lasagna

Description

Indulge in the ultimate comfort food with this mouthwatering lasagna recipe. Layered with love and bursting with flavours, this classic Italian dish is a perfect blend of tender pasta sheets, rich tomateo sauce, savoury ground meat, and a medley of melted cheeses.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound sweet Italian Sausage
  • 3⁄4 pound lean ground beef
  • 1⁄2 cup minced onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1 can of crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cans tomato sauce
  • 2 cans tomato paste
  • 1⁄2 cup water
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 4 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley, divided
  • 1 1⁄2 teaspoons dried basil leaves
  • 1 1⁄2 teaspoons salt, divided, or to taste
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • 1⁄2 teaspoons fennel seeds
  • 1⁄4 teaspoons ground black pepper
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 16 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 3⁄4 pound mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • 3⁄4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
